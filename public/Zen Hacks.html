    ZEN HACKS

    Learn how to plan smart, study smarter, and save time effectively with these proven techniques.

    How to Study Smart

    1️⃣ Focus on active recall and spaced repetition instead of passive reading. These methods strengthen memory retention.

    2️⃣ Create short quizzes or explain topics in your own words to ensure deep understanding instead of memorization.

    How to Plan Correctly

    1️⃣ Start your day with a clear goal and divide tasks into manageable chunks. Use planners or digital apps for daily tracking.

    2️⃣ Plan breaks between study sessions to maintain mental energy and prevent burnout.

    Save Time Effectively

    1️⃣ Use the Pomodoro technique — 25 minutes of study followed by a 5-minute rest. It improves focus and consistency.

    2️⃣ Prioritize tasks with the Eisenhower Matrix: Do first what’s important and urgent, and delegate or delete what’s not.

    Stay Focused

    1️⃣ Create a distraction-free environment — keep your phone away and maintain a tidy workspace.

    2️⃣ Practice short mindfulness breaks to reset your attention and sustain longer focus sessions.

    Mindful Learning

    1️⃣ Apply the Feynman Technique — explain concepts simply to identify knowledge gaps and master topics faster.

    2️⃣ Combine visual aids and notes for dual coding. Mixing diagrams with text enhances long-term memory.
